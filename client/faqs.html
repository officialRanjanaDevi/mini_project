    Order related questions
  • Can I book a bike in advance?
    Yes, you can book a bike in advance. We recommend booking early, especially during peak seasons.
  • Do I need to create an account to rent a bike?
    Yes, creating an account helps us manage your booking and provides a better service experience.
  • Where can I pick up the bike?
    You can pick up the bike at our designated rental locations. The exact address will be provided in your booking confirmation.
  • Can I return the bike at a different location?
    Yes, but additional fees may apply. Please check our one-way rental policy for more information.
  • Do you provide locks and safety gear?
    Yes, locks and basic safety gear such as helmets and lights are provided with your rental.

    Terms and Policies

  • What is your policy on late returns?
    Late returns may incur additional fees. Please inform us if you anticipate being late to avoid penalties.
  • What should I do if I lose the bike key?
    Contact our customer service immediately. There may be a fee for a replacement key.
  • Are there any age restrictions for renting a bike?
    Renters must be at least 18 years old and also they should have driving lisense. Riders under 18 must have parental consent and supervision.
  • Can I extend my rental period?
    Yes, you can extend your rental period, subject to availability. Please contact us as soon as possible to arrange the extension.
  • What should I do if the bike breaks down?
    Contact our customer service immediately. We provide roadside assistance and will help you with repairs or a replacement bike.

    Pricing related questions
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payment options.
  • When will I be charged for the rental?
    You will be charged at the time of booking or at the start of the rental period, depending on the rent of your selected bike and period for wich you have rented.
  • Can I get a refund if I cancel my booking?
    Refund policies vary. Please check our cancellation policy for details on refunds and any applicable fees.
  • Can I get some discount or offers?
    It depends on the season.You can get offers or discounts if there are no travelling seasons.
  • What are the charges if the bike is damaged or any accessories is lost?
    It depends on the bike modal if the bike is damaged and in case of accessories it may cost from 300 rs to 500rs.
